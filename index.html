Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie


Photo by lara604 / Flickr


There's nothing like a classic chocolate chip cookie!

Before we begin, here's what you'll need for one batch:
  • 1 cup of softened salted butter
  • 1 cup of packed light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Let's get to it!

  1. First, preheat your over to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Get a medium bowl to mix together your flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set it aside after.
  3. In a different bowl, cream together your butter and sugar until combined.
  4. In that same bowl, beat in eggs and vanilla for about 1 minute.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips and mix.
  7. Get your baking sheets ready, line them with parchment paper.
  8. Roll approximately 2 or 3 tablespoons of dough at a time into balls and place them on the baking sheet. Make sure they are evenly spaced and prep another baking sheet if you need more room.
  9. When you're ready, put them in them in the oven! Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  10. After removing them from the oven, let them sit for 5 minutes before moving them to a cooling rack.

Enjoy!
